HR Leave Policy

Annual Leave
Full-time employees are eligible for 18 days of annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave should be requested at least five working days in advance through the HR portal. Managers should approve or reject leave requests within two working days.

Sick Leave
Employees are eligible for 10 days of sick leave per calendar year. For sick leave longer than two consecutive working days, the employee must submit a medical certificate to HR within three working days of returning to work.

Casual Leave
Employees may use up to 6 days of casual leave per year for urgent personal matters. Casual leave should be communicated to the reporting manager as early as possible.

Carry Forward Rule
Employees may carry forward up to 5 unused annual leave days into the next calendar year. Any annual leave above 5 unused days will lapse at the end of the year unless an exception is approved by HR.

Leave During Probation
Employees under probation may take sick leave and emergency casual leave. Annual leave during probation requires manager and HR approval.

Public Holidays
Public holidays are announced by HR at the start of every calendar year. If a public holiday falls on a weekend, no compensatory holiday is automatically provided unless announced by management.
