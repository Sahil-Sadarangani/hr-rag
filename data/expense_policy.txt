Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy

Eligibility
Employees can claim reimbursement for business-related expenses that are pre-approved by their reporting manager. Examples include client travel, official meals, conference registration, local transport, and approved software tools.

Submission Deadline
Expense claims must be submitted within 15 calendar days from the date of expense. Claims submitted after 15 days may be rejected unless an exception is approved by Finance.

Required Documents
Every claim must include a valid invoice or receipt, expense date, vendor name, amount, business purpose, and manager approval. Credit card slips alone are not sufficient unless accompanied by a detailed bill.

Travel Expenses
Domestic business travel must be booked according to the company travel guidelines. Economy class is mandatory for domestic flights unless senior management approves an exception.

Meal Expenses
Meal expenses during official travel are reimbursable up to the daily limit announced by Finance. Alcohol is not reimbursable under the standard expense policy.

Payment Timeline
Approved reimbursement claims are usually processed within 7 to 10 working days by Finance.
