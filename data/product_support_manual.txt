Product Support Manual

Support Channels
Customers can contact support through email, live chat, and the customer portal. Priority customers may also receive phone support during business hours.

Ticket Priority Levels
Priority P1 means the production system is unavailable for most users. P2 means a major feature is degraded but a workaround exists. P3 means a minor issue affects limited users. P4 means a general question, documentation request, or feature suggestion.

Response Time Targets
P1 tickets should receive first response within 30 minutes. P2 tickets should receive first response within 2 business hours. P3 tickets should receive first response within 1 business day. P4 tickets should receive first response within 2 business days.

Escalation
If a P1 issue remains unresolved for more than 2 hours, the support engineer must escalate it to the engineering on-call team and notify the customer success manager.

Knowledge Base
Support engineers should document recurring issues in the knowledge base. Each article should include symptoms, cause, resolution steps, affected versions, and validation checks.
